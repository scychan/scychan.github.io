I'm a Staff Research Scientist at Google DeepMind, working on:
1. **Future impacts of AI**. I'm building a new team trying to understand the potential societal impacts of AI. Reach out if you're interested!

2. **AI to empower humans**. AI can be used to augment human potential and human capital, e.g. via [education](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.12687) and the [information ecosystem](https://deepmind.google/blog/exploring-the-context-of-online-images-with-backstory/), if we do it responsibly and inclusively.

3. **Science of AI**. We try to understand modern AI systems as an object of study, much as neuroscientists and cognitive scientists try to understand the brain. A lot of my work focuses on in-context learning.


In my previous lives, I've done research in robotics, human memory and decision-making, and astrophysics. I've also done brain surgeries, built laser-optics rigs, and pitched to VCs.
